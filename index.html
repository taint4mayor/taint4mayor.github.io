Goals & Achievements

Taint is a winner

A track record of reproducing success

The word "Taint" and "success" have become so intertwined that they are almost synonymous with each other. He is a man of many success stories, however, this has not distracted Taint from his love of helping people and watching them blossom successful and valued members of our society.

At the heart of Taint's work in the city is the escalator to success. The goal of the 10 step program is to take on people who are perhaps, misguided, and to escalate them to success. The escalator has proved very successful. 90% of clients rate it as one of the top 3 most significant experiences of their lives, many of its clients have gone to become supervisors, managers, business owners, founders, co-founders, top salespeople, actors, Bible class teachers, ministers, and ministry directors.

What this city needs is someone with experience taking in deprived, wanting and unsuccessful people/communities and turning them into beautiful butterflies. I can think of no better man for the job than Taint. It's time. For Taint.

Our Mission

Bringing the community together

One of Taint's primary objectives is to bring the community together, foster new relationships and to bring the fun levels to an all time high in the city. Therefore, as your Mayor Taint will be focusing on incresing the frequency and partipation in high quality events. Whether they be sports events, entertaintment events, or any other kinds of events.

Bolstering the emergency services

When conducting surveys across the city we found many people were disappointed with emergency services coverage, particularly EMS. Therefore as Mayor, Taint will be deploying creative strategies and programs in order improve coverage of emergency services.

Voice of the people

There is nothing more important in a community than making sure that citzens feel like their voice is heard. Sometimes people have an issue but don't bring it up in community meetings. Therefore as Mayor, Taint will make it a priority to listen to peoples issues and concerns in the city and take an active role in community meetings.
